The brand building studio turning brands into iconic companies — from Udaipur, India.

Virtue & Wisdom is a brand building company in Udaipur, Rajasthan, founded by Vidit Meghwal — driven by the pursuit of excellence. We collaborate with visionary companies across India and abroad, transforming their brands into beacons of inspiration and impact, with a keen focus on authenticity and craft.

We work hand-in-hand with founders, CEOs, and executive teams to unlock the brand's untapped potential. Through strategy, identity, marketing, and experience, we build brands worth rallying around — and the systems that keep them compounding. Our deepest work happens in specific sectors we know well: marble, hospitality, cafés, and jewellery.
