The framework

The nine steps, in order.

— 01 Brand purpose Why you do what you do — beyond making money. We use the contribution-and-impact formula.
— 02 Brand vision Where you're going. Now, five years, ten years, fifteen.
— 03 Brand values The principles that decide how you behave when nobody's watching.
— 04 Target audience Goals, problems, impact, desires. The customer sketch you'll build everything around.
— 05 Market analysis The two extremes you choose to stand for. Where the gap is. Why anyone should care.
— 06 Awareness goals Which channels are worth the cost — and which to skip. Plotted against impact and difficulty.
The next three steps — personality, voice, tagline — and the synthesised one-page strategy unlock with the report. ₹2,000 (≈$25).
— 07 Brand personality The archetypal mix. 70% one, 30% another. The rules of attitude that follow from it.
— 08 Brand voice Where you sit on humour, formality, respect, enthusiasm. The "we are X but not Y" formulas.
— 09 Brand tagline Imperative, descriptive, superlative, provocative, specific. We help you find the one that fits.
